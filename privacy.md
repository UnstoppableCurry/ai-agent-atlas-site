# Privacy Policy

**AI Agent Atlas** does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

The app runs entirely on your local machine. There are no servers,
no analytics, no SDK telemetry, and no crash reporting service.
Nothing leaves your computer.

## What data does the app store?

- Theme preference (paper / ink)
- Language preference (English / 中文)
- Font scale value (0.8x – 1.4x)
- Whether you have seen the onboarding tour

All of these are stored in your local browser-style storage and never
leave the app.
