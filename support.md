## Frequently Asked Questions

### The app shows "unable to verify developer" on first launch.
Right-click the app in Finder → **Open** → click **Open** in the dialog. macOS will remember your choice and the warning won't appear again.

### Does it work offline?
Yes, 100%. The app contains all 35 architecture nodes, 13 scenarios, and every code snippet built-in. No internet connection required at any point.

### Is there an Intel version?
Yes, Apple Silicon (arm64) and Intel (x64) Macs are both supported via the universal binary on the Mac App Store.

### Can I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple, not by us. Use [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com) within 14 days of purchase.

### How do I change the language?
Click the **EN / 中文** button in the top of the left sidebar. The setting persists across app launches.

### How do I make the icons bigger?
Drag the **A · A** font scale slider in the left sidebar. Range 80%–140%; the architecture map and all icons scale together.

### How do I hide the sidebar?
Click the **‹** button in the top-right of the sidebar header. To bring it back, click the **›** button on the left edge of the screen (vertically centered).

### How do I see the onboarding tour again?
Click the **?** Help button in the settings strip on the left sidebar.

## Reporting bugs

If you find a bug, please email us with:
1. macOS version (Apple menu → About This Mac)
2. App version (visible in the About dialog)
3. What you did that triggered the bug
4. (Optional) A screenshot
